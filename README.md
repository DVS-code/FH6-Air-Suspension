# FH6 Air Suspension

Real-time air suspension for **Forza Horizon 6**. Slam your car to the ground and lift it back up on a keybind in real time.

## Features

- **Air suspension slam / lift** — one keybind smoothly drops the car onto the ground; press again to raise it back up.
- **Custom keybind** — rebind the slam key to whatever you want; your choice is saved.
- **Adjustable slam strength** — 1.0×–1.5×, dial in exactly how low it sits.
- **Adjustable drop speed** — 1–8 second ease, from a quick deflate to a slow drop.
- **Pressure-release sound** — a real air-release clip plays on the way down. Toggle on/off.
- **Ride-height limits editor** — search your car and set custom Min/Max ride-height limits (applies once you go to the suspenion category in the upgrades menu).

## How to use

1. Launch **Forza Horizon 6** (offline recommended).
2. Run **`FH6AirSuspension.exe`** (approve the admin prompt).
3. Click **Connect**.
4. Press your keybind (default **F8**) to slam — tune **Slam strength** and **Drop speed** to taste.

## Requirements

- Windows 10 / 11
- Runs **as administrator**

## Notes

- **Offline / single-player only.** Do not use in any online mode (Rivals, multiplayer, leaderboards) — you risk a ban. Use at your own risk.
